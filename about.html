Our Story

Loxion Legends is a youth-driven sports initiative in the kasi, created to empower young stars who love sports. We give kids from ekasi a platform to shine through football, netball, and other activities. Many talented learners are left behind due to lack of support or resources — that is where we step in.

Our Core Values

Who We Serve

We serve township youth aged 7–25, male and female, who love sports and want to grow.

We teach life skills, teamwork, and offer opportunities for tournaments and mentorship.

Even with limited resources, we help young stars maximize their potential.

"Re batla go boost’a confidence ya bana ba kasi who’ve got game but no platform."
